\begin{frame}{Learning Outcomes}
    By the end of this session, you should be able to:
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Understand the structure and working of Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs).
        \item Recognize various RNN architectures (One-to-Many, Many-to-One, Many-to-Many).
        \item Explain the concept of shared parameters in RNNs.
        \item Explain why RNNs suffer from vanishing and exploding gradients.
        \item Understand how Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) and Gated Recurrent Unit (GRU) architectures solve these problems.
        \item Compare LSTM and GRU architectures in terms of performance and complexity.
        \item Recognize limitations and future directions in sequence modeling.
    \end{itemize}
  \end{frame}
