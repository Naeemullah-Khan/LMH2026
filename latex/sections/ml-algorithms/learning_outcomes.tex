\section{Learning Outcomes}
% --- Slide: Learning Outcomes ---
\begin{frame}{Learning Outcomes}
    By the end of this session, you will be able to:
    
    \vspace{0.5em}
    \begin{enumerate}
        \item Navigate the ML Toolbox and distinguish between Linear, Distance-based, Kernel-based, and Tree-based models.
        \item Understand Model Intuition and explain how these algorithms learn (e.g., Maximizing Margin in SVM, Greedy Splits in Trees).
        \item Understand Ensembling and contrast Bagging (Random Forest) vs. Boosting (Gradient Boosting) and why they reduce error.
        \item Understand the advantages and disadvantages of each algorithm.
    \end{enumerate}
    
\end{frame}
