\section{Learning Outcomes}
\begin{frame}{Learning Outcomes}
    By the end of this session, you should be able to:
    \vspace{0.5em}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Define and distinguish Large Reasoning Models (LRMs) from simpler pattern-matching LLMs.
        \item Understand advanced reasoning techniques, including Chain-of-Thought (CoT), Tree-of-Thought (ToT), and self-consistency methods.
        \item Explain how instruction tuning and reinforcement learning (RL) can refine and enhance reasoning abilities in language models.
        \item Grasp the core concepts of Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architectures, including gating, routing mechanisms, trade-offs, benefits, and limitations.
        \item Critically assess future directions and identify open problems in the field of reasoning with language models.
    \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
