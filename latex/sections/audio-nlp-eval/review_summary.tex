\section{Summary}

\begin{frame}[allowframebreaks]{Final Summary: Key Takeaways}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Speech is a rich modality for natural language processing, bridging sound and meaning.

    \item Audio features such as \textbf{MFCC}, \textbf{Mel}, and \textbf{Log-Mel} form the foundation for ASR and TTS systems.

    \item The spoken interaction cycle involves \textbf{ASR → SLU → TTS}, enabling complete end-to-end communication.

    \item \textbf{Evaluation} is crucial: combine automated metrics (WER, BLEU, ROUGE) with human and LLM-based assessments.

    \item Future directions:
    \begin{itemize}
        \item More \textbf{end-to-end models} for robust and expressive speech understanding/synthesis.
        \item Improved, \textbf{semantic and human-centered evaluation metrics}.
        \item Stronger \textbf{ethical safeguards} against misuse.
    \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}

\end{frame}
