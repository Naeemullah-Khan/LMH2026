\begin{frame}[t]{Learning Outcomes}
  After this session, you will be able to:
  \begin{itemize}
    \item[\textcolor{red}{$\blacktriangleright$}] Explain and compare different fine-tuning methods for LLMs
    \item[\textcolor{red}{$\blacktriangleright$}] Understand the pipeline of Supervised Fine-Tuning and RLHF
    \item[\textcolor{red}{$\blacktriangleright$}] Describe PPO (Proximal Policy Optimization) and DPO (Direct Preference Optimization)
    \item[\textcolor{red}{$\blacktriangleright$}] Apply Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA) and Quantized LoRA for efficient tuning
    \item[\textcolor{red}{$\blacktriangleright$}] Analyze trade-offs, limitations, and future directions of LLM adaptation
  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
