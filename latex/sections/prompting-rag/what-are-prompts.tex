\begin{frame}{What are prompts?}
  \begin{itemize}
    \item[\textcolor{red}{$\blacktriangleright$}] \textbf{Prompts} involve instructions and context passed to a language model to achieve a desired task.
    \vspace{0.5em}
    \item[\textcolor{red}{$\blacktriangleright$}] \textbf{Prompt engineering} is the practice of developing and optimizing prompts to efficiently use language models (LMs) for a variety of applications.
    \vspace{0.5em}
    \item[\textcolor{red}{$\blacktriangleright$}] Prompt engineering is a useful skill for AI engineers and researchers to improve and efficiently use language models.
  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
